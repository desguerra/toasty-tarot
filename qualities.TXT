Cardinal:
+ initiator and leader signs
+ represent start of their respective seasons
+ Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn
Fixed:
+ reliable and stable signs
+ dedicated with strong follow through
+ Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius, Taurus
Mutable:
+ adaptable and versitile signs
+ represent end of their respective seasons
+ Sagittarius, Pisces, Gemini, Virgo
